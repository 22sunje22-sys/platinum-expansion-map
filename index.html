Explore the shortlist like a working map

Start from the world view, then drill down market by market. This is meant to feel interactive, not like a static slide.

2 Use your trackpad, mouse wheel, or pinch gesture to zoom. Drag the map to pan around.
3 Use filters above to narrow by region or status, then move through visible markets with Prev and Next.
Tip: press Esc or click empty map space to zoom back out.
